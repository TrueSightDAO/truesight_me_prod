Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TrueSight DAO, how to get involved, and how our community governance works.

What is TrueSight DAO?

TrueSight DAO is a decentralized autonomous organization dedicated to building a better world based on compassion. We use open-source principles to create social ventures that drive social and environmental justice. Our community earns governance tokens by contributing to these ventures, enabling transparent, community-led decision making. Learn more on our About Us page.

How do I join TrueSight DAO?

Joining TrueSight DAO is open to anyone who shares our values of mindfulness, compassion, and equanimity. Start by exploring our Web App to see how you can contribute. You can also join our Telegram community to connect with other members, participate in community proposals, and learn about ongoing initiatives.

What are TDG tokens?

TDG (TrueSight DAO Governance) tokens are governance tokens that represent your stake and voting power in the DAO. You earn TDG tokens by contributing to our community initiatives, such as building tools, supporting cacao farmers, participating in tree planting programs, or contributing to governance discussions. The more you contribute, the more TDG tokens you earn, giving you greater influence in community decisions.

For developers and technical contributors: Our Tokenomics Repository contains the open-source code and automation scripts that power our ecosystem.

How do I earn TDG tokens?

You can earn TDG tokens by contributing to TrueSight DAO initiatives in various ways:

  • Contributing to our projects (Agroverse, Sunmint, Edgar)
  • Participating in governance discussions and voting
  • Building tools and solutions for the community
  • Supporting our social ventures and impact initiatives
  • Helping with documentation, outreach, and community building

Track your contributions and TDG earnings in our Contributions Record.

How does governance work?

TrueSight DAO uses a community-led governance model where members vote on proposals using their TDG tokens. Your voting power is proportional to your TDG holdings, which reflect your contributions to the DAO. You can view and participate in active community proposals, and see our Community Leaders who help guide our direction.

What are the different projects?

TrueSight DAO has three main ongoing projects:

  • Agroverse - Connecting small-scale cacao farmers to global markets with transparent tokenomics
  • Sunmint - Reforesting the Amazon through agroforestry and carbon sequestration
  • Edgar - An AI agent that tokenizes contributions and streamlines cacao sales

How do I track my contributions?

You can view your contribution history and TDG earnings in our Contributions Record. This transparent ledger shows all your contributions to the DAO and the corresponding TDG tokens earned. You can also view Community Engagement Logs to see detailed activity across our community platforms.

Where can I learn more?

There are several resources to learn more about TrueSight DAO:

  • Read our Whitepaper for detailed information about our mission and tokenomics
  • Explore our Blog for updates and insights
  • For technical details and code, visit our Tokenomics Repository (for developers)
  • Join our Telegram community to connect with members
  • Check out our GitHub to see our open-source projects

How can I get support?

If you have questions that aren't answered here, you can:

  • Join our Telegram community and ask in the chat
  • Participate in community discussions
  • Explore our Blog for detailed guides and updates
