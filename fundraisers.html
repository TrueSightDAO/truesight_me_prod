How it works

How a fundraiser works

Part 1

Donation comes in

Supporter donates via Stripe (USD) on the fundraiser's site. The DAO captures the charge ID, donor email, gross, fees, and net.

Part 2

Currency conversion

USD is wired to a Wise (or similar) account and converted to the recipient's local currency. The Wise receipt is attached to a signed [CURRENCY CONVERSION EVENT] on the DAO ledger.

Part 3

Disbursement to partner

Local-currency funds are transferred to the program partner (e.g. via Pix in Brazil). The Pix receipt is attached to a signed [DAO Inventory Expense Event].

Part 4

Publicly auditable

The full ledger — every donation in, every conversion, every disbursement — is published as JSON to GitHub and rendered by the fundraiser's explorer subdomain. No accounts, no private dashboards.
