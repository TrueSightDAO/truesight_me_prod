Governors

Governors are system-chosen, not nominated. A formula on the Governors tab of the Main Ledger ranks every contributor by total TDG issued over the trailing 180 days (excluding passive USDT-flow lines) and seats the top 10. The window slides daily, so the roster is technically continuous; the formal acknowledgment cadence is each Spring Equinox, Summer Solstice, Autumn Equinox, and Winter Solstice (~every 3 months). No campaigning, no self-nomination — the only path in is sustained contribution.

What governors can do

Privileges (from the whitepaper)

  • Editing rights to the Main Ledger.
  • Editing rights to the scoring rubric.
  • Administrative rights on the DAO’s WhatsApp / Telegram channels.

Duties

  • Around each solstice and equinox, review Edgar-scored DApp submissions and approve consolidated ledger entries.
  • Resolve any notarization disputes surfaced by DAO members.

Governor-only DApp surfaces

  • Permissions viewer + editor — read or change the DApp permission matrix (who can see / use which surface).
  • Add new contributor — register a new DAO contributor with name + email so they can sign and submit.
