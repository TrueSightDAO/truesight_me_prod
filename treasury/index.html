Why these three lines and not "all ledgers"?

Treasury Balance is what the DAO actually owns: cash and stablecoin on the books, plus its equity stake in each managed ledger. Assets Under Management (AUM) is a separate metric that includes investor capital sitting in those same ledgers — see /aum (when shipped) or the headline AUM tile on truesight.me.

Each AGL ledger is a separate Balance sheet with rows for every stakeholder (investors, the DAO, the operator). This page sums only the rows where Column A = "TrueSight DAO" and Column C = "USD".
