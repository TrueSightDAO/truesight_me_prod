Troubleshooting Governance Tokens Not Showing in Phantom Wallet

June 27, 2025 by Community TrueSight

Introduction

If you're a DAO member and your governance tokens aren't appearing in your Phantom wallet, don’t worry! This is a common issue that can often be resolved with a few simple steps. Below, we’ll guide you through the process to ensure your tokens are visible and accessible.


Why Tokens Might Not Show Up

Governance tokens may not display due to several reasons, including incorrect network selection, unverified token contracts, or wallet synchronization issues. Let’s walk through the steps to fix this.


Steps to Resolve the Issue


Step 1: Verify Network Selection

  • Open your Phantom wallet and click on the token list settings


Step 2: Click on Manage Tokens

  • This is where you override the tokens display list management

Step 3: Search for our Token

  • Insert our Token address in the search bar: 3wmsJkKWLdFT4tF4rG8zUZQ8M4hKUDtDuJW8q6i9KbgF


Step 4: Enable the Token

  • Toggle the switch next to the token to enable it in your wallet, as shown in the screenshot.


Step 5: Close and Reopen the Wallet

  • After enabling the token, close the token details window by clicking the "Close" button.

  • Refresh your wallet and reopen the app or browser extension to ensure the changes take effect.


Step 6: Check Token Balance

  • Return to the "Tokens" section and verify that the governance token now appears with the correct balance.

  • If the balance is still "null" or incorrect, ensure the token has been properly transferred to your wallet address.


Conclusion

By following these steps, you should be able to see your governance tokens in your Phantom wallet. If issues continue, it might be worth consulting with your DAO administrators or the Phantom support team. Happy governing!
