About this number

AUM is the gross rollup of every line in treasury-cache/dao_offchain_treasury.json — the same source feeding the inline breakdown that used to live on the landing page. Each ledger reports its own balance; this page sums them.

The headline AUM stat on truesight.me is computed separately by GAS from the same underlying ledgers and may differ slightly due to currency-rate snapshots and which item types each side includes. Both surfaces are honest reads of the same data with different rollup rules — the per-ledger and per-currency sums below always match each other (same JSON, two pivots).

The USD Treasury Balance is a stricter cut: off-chain cash + USDT vault + the DAO's equity stake in each AGL (not investor capital). AUM here includes the investor capital and the broader inventory.
