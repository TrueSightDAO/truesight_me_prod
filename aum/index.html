About this number

AUM is computed from each AGL's Asset section (what the ledger holds), not its Equity section (stakeholder claims). When books reconcile these totals are equal per ledger, but the asset side is the semantically correct read for AUM and removes any risk of double-counting items also tracked on the Main Ledger.

The USD Treasury Balance is a stricter cut: off-chain cash + USDT vault + the DAO's equity stake in each AGL only. AUM here is the gross picture — the DAO's equity plus investor capital plus all the cacao + packaging + other inventory across every managed ledger.

Source: GAS ?type=aum_breakdown endpoint, cached via PropertiesService. Refreshed on every updateTotalDAOAssetOnWix cron run as a side effect.
